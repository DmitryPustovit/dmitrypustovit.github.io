    A Reflection

    The first draft of my narrative did not really fall under the category of a narrative. Although I close a topic that was in fact quite important to me, music, it was a rather difficult topic to work with. My revisions focused on attempting to improve the flow and add more of a personal touch to the paper. I find it difficult to edit and revise a paper that I have already written so improving my narrative posed a rather interesting challenge by itself. I added more of my personality but found it difficult without changing the topic entirely.
