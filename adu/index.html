What is an Advaiti?

An Adviti(or an ADU) is a unit of messurement developed to approximate the height of a traffic light. One Advaiti is 5 Feet and 3 inches. A common problem with the MANY other people based units of messurement is that the person may grow causing the unit to change. This is with opsAdviti(oADU) which is a unit that is a fixed heighr or exactly 3.39583 feet.
